## 3. Basics of Statistics

It's possible to provide answers to important inquiries about data that use a collection of statistical methods and tools termed statistics. It is therefore divided into two groups:

 

 


 
### ●  	Descriptive:

To provide techniques to simplify data by transforming unstructured data into clear data that is simple to distribute and convey, we use _Descriptive Statistics._

 

 


 
### ●  	Inferential

_Inferential Statistics_ thus helps in providing tools for analyzing operations containing smaller samples of data and then making judgments that apply to the whole population (entire domain).


	    	

Therefore, statistics and data science are both fields of research that are highly integrated. Although statisticians contribute mostly to the development, evaluation, and implementation of prediction models, it serves as a fundamental requirement for practical machine learning.

 


## 4. Connectivity of ‘Statistics’ & ‘Data Science’

 

The basis behind data science involves statistics. When we lack a strong knowledge of statistics principles, we can't utilize data science effectively to overcome real-world problems.

Several factors make mastering statistics complicated. I'm pointing to precisely stated theories, greek notation, and mathematical equations, which make it hard to become fascinated by the subject.

 

Statistics are essential to problem-solving in all relevant areas and fields, from exploratory data analysis to constructing experiments for hypothesis testing.

Someone who wishes to acquire a complete understanding of data science must learn how statistical techniques serve as a foundation for classification and regression algorithms, how statistics permits us to benefit from data, and how it assists in the analysis of unlabeled data.
